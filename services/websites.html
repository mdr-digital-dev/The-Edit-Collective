FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects launch within 2-4 weeks depending on scope. A single-page Starter site can be live in as little as 7 business days, while a multi-page Growth build typically takes 3-4 weeks including revisions and content integration.

What's included in the monthly fee?

Your monthly fee covers reliable hosting, SSL certificate, regular security updates, daily backups, uptime monitoring, and basic content edits. Growth plans also include analytics reporting and priority support for faster response times.

Can I update the site myself?

Absolutely. We build sites with user-friendly content management systems so you can easily update text, images, and blog posts on your own. We also provide a short training walkthrough after launch so you feel confident making changes.

What if I need something custom?

We love custom work. Whether it's e-commerce, booking systems, membership portals, or unique interactive features, we'll scope out exactly what you need and provide a transparent quote. Just reach out and we'll put together a tailored plan.
